# Human-Centered AI Research

This directory contains projects focused on **human-centered artificial intelligence**, including safe AI systems, responsible AI, explainable healthcare AI, social-impact computing, AI-assisted reporting systems, and reproducible benchmarks for safety-aware machine learning.

## Research Theme

Human-centered AI focuses on designing intelligent systems that are not only accurate, but also:

- Safe
- Explainable
- Accountable
- Transparent
- Reproducible
- Useful for real-world human needs
- Sensitive to social, clinical, and ethical contexts

This research direction connects **machine learning, natural language processing, medical AI, explainable AI, and responsible AI system design**.
