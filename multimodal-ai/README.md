# Multimodal AI Research

This directory contains research projects focused on **multimodal artificial intelligence**, including vision-language learning, video understanding, medical report summarization, and multimodal fusion.

## Research Theme

Multimodal AI aims to build intelligent systems that can jointly understand information from multiple sources such as:

- Text
- Images
- Videos
- Clinical reports
- Visual questions
- Language instructions
- Contextual signals

My work in this area focuses on **vision-language reasoning, multimodal attention, clinical AI, video-based affective understanding, and human-centered intelligent systems**.
