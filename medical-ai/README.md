# Medical AI Research

This directory contains research projects focused on **medical artificial intelligence**, including disease screening, medical image classification, clinical time-series forecasting, transfer learning, and deep learning-based clinical decision support.

## Research Theme

Medical AI aims to build computational systems that can support early disease detection, clinical screening, risk prediction, and healthcare decision-making.

This research direction focuses on:

- Medical image classification
- Clinical time-series forecasting
- Disease screening
- Transfer learning
- Deep feature extraction
- Medical decision support
- Explainable and reproducible healthcare AI
